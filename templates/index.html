Workflow

What happens after upload

  1. The image is resized to the saved model's expected input size.
  2. The CNN predicts one of the colorectal histology tissue classes.
  3. Grad-CAM highlights the most influential image regions.
  4. The app returns the prediction, confidence, and heatmap overlay.

This interface is for project demonstration and research use. It is not a substitute for clinical judgment or pathology review.
